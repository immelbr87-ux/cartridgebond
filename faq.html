The Bond is our timeline feature. Most marketplaces force you to sell now or buy now. The Bond lets you commit today to a trade in 30, 60, or 90 days.

As a buyer, you can lock in a guaranteed buyback price the moment you purchase - knowing exactly what your "true cost of play" will be. As a seller, you can pre-arrange the resale before you've even finished playing. Researched pricing makes it possible.

You submit a game with your locked-in price, condition, and timeline. We review it manually, then match it to a compatible buyer or seller in the area. Once matched, both parties get an email with the meetup details.

The price is fixed at submission - no door-side renegotiation, no surprise changes.

Most matches happen within 48 hours. During the Milwaukee beta we manually review every submission, which means real human eyes on every listing - and a slightly slower turnaround than the eventual auto-matched system.

Prices are based on current secondary market rates - what games actually sell for between private parties locally and on eBay (sold listings, not asking prices). We cross-reference Wisconsin eBay sold data, PriceCharting Complete-condition values, and observed local activity.

Sellers lock in their price at submission and it does not change at the meetup.
