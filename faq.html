Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying and selling on CartridgeBond.
How It Works
CartridgeBond matches local Nintendo Switch game buyers and sellers in Milwaukee and the North Shore. You submit what you have or want, lock in your price and condition terms, and we find someone compatible in your area. No shipping, no strangers from across the country.
Price is locked before you meet. On Facebook Marketplace, buyers often show up and try to negotiate at the door. On CartridgeBond, both parties commit to a price before anyone moves. The meetup is just to verify the game - not to haggle.
You submit a game with your price, condition, and timeline. A real person reviews every submission and looks for a compatible buyer or seller nearby. When we find a match, we email both parties with contact info. You coordinate the meetup directly. No bots, no auto-matching.
We review submissions within 24 hours. How quickly you get matched depends on local demand. Mario Kart and Zelda move fastest. If no immediate match exists, your submission stays active until we find one.
