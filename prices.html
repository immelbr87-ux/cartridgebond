How these prices work

What "A1 condition" actually means

Every cartridge that trades on CartridgeBond must meet our A1 standard. This is what gives us the leverage to lock in prices - the buyer knows exactly what they're getting before money changes hands. A1 means:

  • Cartridge in original case - CIB required, no third-party cases or missing cases
  • Plays without errors - boots, runs, saves work normally
  • No deep scratches affecting gameplay - light surface scuffs on the case are okay
  • Label fully intact - clean cartridge pins, no major label damage
  • Original inserts included where applicable - manual, map, slipcover when the game shipped with one

Sellers who can't meet these standards are filtered out before any match. Buyers don't have to inspect or take chances.
