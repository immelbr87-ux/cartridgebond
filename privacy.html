Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025  ·  CartridgeBond Beta

Plain-English summary: We collect only what we need to match you with a buyer or seller. We don't sell your data. We don't spam. You can ask us to delete your info at any time by emailing us.

1. What We Collect

When you submit a form on CartridgeBond, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number (optional)
  • Your zip code
  • Game title(s), asking price(s), and timeline preferences
  • Any notes you voluntarily include

We may also collect basic analytics data (pages visited, time on site, traffic source) through Google Analytics if enabled. This data is aggregated and not linked to individual identities.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Match you with a compatible buyer or seller
  • Contact you to coordinate a local exchange
  • Follow up on your submission status
  • Send a post-trade rating request (optional)

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to your CartridgeBond submission.

3. Who We Share It With

We share only what is necessary to complete a match:

  • Your matched counterpart — first name and general zip code area only, shared at the time of match notification
  • Google Sheets — submissions are written directly to a private Google Sheet accessible only to CartridgeBond's founder via an automated Google Apps Script

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your submission data for up to 12 months after your last interaction with CartridgeBond, or until you request deletion — whichever comes first. If no match is found within your stated window, your data is removed from our active matching queue.

5. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw from the matching queue

To exercise any of these rights, email cartridgebond@gmail.com. We will respond within 5 business days.

6. Children's Privacy

CartridgeBond requires users to be 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a minor has submitted information through our platform, please contact us immediately and we will remove it.

7. Cookies & Analytics

CartridgeBond may use Google Analytics to understand general traffic patterns (pages visited, referral sources, device types). This data is anonymous and aggregated. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or retargeting. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of CartridgeBond after changes are posted constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: cartridgebond@gmail.com
