PRIVACY POLICY

What information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Last updated: January 2026

CartridgeBond takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

WHAT WE COLLECT

  • Name — used to personalize communications and identify you to your match
  • Email address — used to send you submission confirmations and match notifications
  • Phone number — optional, used only if you choose to share it with a match
  • Zip code — used to find matches in your geographic area
  • Game and price information — used to find compatible matches
  • Meetup preference — used to find matches near your preferred area

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To match you with a compatible buyer or seller in your area
  • To send you submission confirmations and match notifications
  • To improve the CartridgeBond matching process

WHAT WE SHARE

When we find a match, we share your first name and email address with your matched party only. We do not share your phone number, zip code, or any other information without your explicit consent.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your information with advertisers or third-party marketers.

DATA STORAGE

Your submission information is stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to CartridgeBond. We use Google Analytics to understand general site usage (page views, traffic sources). No personally identifiable information is sent to Google Analytics.

YOUR RIGHTS

  • Request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us
  • Request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Opt out of any future communications

COOKIES

We use Google Analytics which sets cookies for traffic analysis. No other cookies are set by CartridgeBond directly.

CONTACT

Privacy questions or data requests: cartridgebond@gmail.com. We respond to all requests within 7 days.
