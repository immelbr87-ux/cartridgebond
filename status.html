What Each Status Means
🔄

ACTIVE

Your submission is in our system and we are looking for a match. A real person reviews every submission within 24 hours.

What to do: Nothing yet. We will email you the moment a match is found. Most matches happen within 48 hours.
🎯

MATCHED

We found a compatible buyer or seller. You should have received an email with their first name and contact info.

What to do: Reply to that email to coordinate a meetup. Use a public location. Inspect the game before paying. See our Safe Meetup Guide.
✓

COMPLETE

Trade confirmed by both parties. You are done.

What to do: If this was your first trade and you were one of the first 25 users, your founder badge is locked in. Tell a friend - the more people, the faster the next match.
