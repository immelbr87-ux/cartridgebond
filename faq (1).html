How It Works
CartridgeBond matches Nintendo Switch game buyers and sellers with prices locked in before anyone meets. We are launching in Milwaukee and the North Shore so we can prove the model with local meetups, then opening nationally with shipping and escrow shortly after. You submit what you have or want, lock in your price and condition, and we find a compatible match.
Soon. The Milwaukee beta is how we prove the model — locked pricing, real matches, real trades — without the complexity of shipping and escrow. Once we have a track record we are opening nationally with full shipping support and escrow protection. If you are outside Milwaukee, join the waitlist and we will email you the moment we expand to your area.
Price is locked before you meet. On Facebook Marketplace, buyers often show up and try to negotiate at the door. On CartridgeBond, both parties commit to a price before anyone moves. The meetup is just to verify the game - not to haggle.
You submit a game with your price, condition, and timeline. A real person reviews every submission and looks for a compatible buyer or seller nearby. When we find a match, we email both parties with contact info. You coordinate the meetup directly. No bots, no auto-matching.

Pricing
Think of CartridgeBond as Certified Pre-Owned for Switch games — not a garage sale. Prices are based on current secondary market rates — what games actually sell for between private parties locally and on eBay (sold listings, not asking prices). We cross-reference Wisconsin eBay sold data, PriceCharting Complete-condition values, and observed local activity. Sellers lock in their price at submission and it does not change at the meetup — that is the CartridgeBond standard.

Condition & Games
A1 means: game plays perfectly, original case included, no scratches that affect gameplay, cartridge pins are clean. Minor surface scuffs on the case are acceptable. Missing case does not qualify. We require A1 for all current listings.
We currently support 25+ Nintendo Switch titles including Mario Kart 8 Deluxe, both Zelda titles, Smash Bros Ultimate, all main-line Pokemon games, Animal Crossing, Splatoon, Mario Party, and more — see the full list on the Game Values page. Unlisted games can be submitted using the custom search field on the form. More platforms are coming as we expand.
