The rules of the road for using CartridgeBond.

Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. What CartridgeBond is

CartridgeBond is a connection platform that matches Nintendo Switch game buyers and sellers. We do not own, store, or transfer ownership of any games. Every transaction occurs directly between matched parties. CartridgeBond is not a party to your transaction.

2. Eligibility

You must be at least 13 years old to use CartridgeBond. By submitting a listing, you confirm you meet this minimum age. If you are under 18, you must have a parent or guardian's permission to use the service.

3. Listings and pricing

You agree that submitted prices are binding once locked. Both parties commit to the price at submission, and renegotiation at the meetup is not permitted. If your counterparty attempts to change terms, you should leave the meetup and contact us.

You confirm that any item you list meets the stated condition standard (currently A1 - like new, original case, plays perfectly).

4. Meetups and safety

You are responsible for your own safety at any meetup. We recommend reading the safe meetup guide before your first trade. CartridgeBond does not chaperone or supervise meetups and is not liable for any incidents that occur during a transaction.

5. Beta service

CartridgeBond is currently in beta. The service is provided as-is. Features, pricing methodology, condition tiers, and platform fees may change with reasonable notice. We will give 30 days notice before introducing platform fees.

6. Founder spots

The first 25 users to complete a trade qualify for "founder pricing" - free platform fees on all future trades, even after fees are introduced. Founder status is non-transferable and tied to the verified email of the user.

7. Prohibited conduct

  • Listing counterfeit, stolen, or non-functional cartridges
  • Misrepresenting condition
  • Renegotiating prices at the meetup
  • Harassing other users
  • Using CartridgeBond for any unlawful purpose

Violations may result in account termination and forfeiture of founder status.

8. Limitation of liability

CartridgeBond, its operators, and affiliates are not liable for any direct, indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from use of the service, including damaged or non-functional games, no-show counterparties, or any incident at a meetup.

9. Changes to these terms

We may update these terms from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email to active users. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
